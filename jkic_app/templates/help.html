REQUIREMENTS FOR THE ADMISSION

The candidates are advised to carry the following documents at the time of admission to avoid any inconvenience.

  • The original certificates of the examinations viz High-school, intermediate and graduation etc.
  • Four passport-sized photographs , latest and facing in front.
  • Transfer Certificate(T.C.),Migration certificate (M.C)and Character Certificate(C.C.)in original.
  • No benefit will be given if the required certificate of SC, ST, OBC, Physically challanged, Freedom Fighter,Ex-serviceman etc.are not produced in original at the time of the admission.
  • The certificates of sports N.C.C., N.S.S., Scouts/ Guide, Ranger/ Rover, Staff-Ward in original and their self-attested photocopies.(If applicable)
  • Required fees (according to the class, subject or group).
